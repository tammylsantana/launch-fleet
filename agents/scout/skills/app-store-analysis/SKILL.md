# App Store Analysis Skill

Analyze the Apple App Store to identify competitors, market gaps, and positioning opportunities.

## Competitor Analysis Framework
For each competitor app, evaluate:
1. App Store rating and review count
2. Pricing model (free, freemium, paid, subscription)
3. Last update date (is it actively maintained?)
4. Screenshot quality and messaging
5. Top positive review themes
6. Top negative review themes (these reveal the gap)
7. Category ranking if available

## Market Gap Identification
Look for patterns in negative reviews across multiple competitors:
- "I wish this app had..."
- "The only thing missing is..."
- "I would pay for..."
- "Why doesn't anyone make..."

These reveal real market opportunities.

## Category Intelligence
Know the current state of each major App Store category:
- Health & Fitness: dominated by subscription apps, $9.99-19.99/month typical
- Productivity: freemium with premium tiers, widget support expected
- Social Networking: free with in-app purchases, privacy focus trending
- Education: subscription or one-time purchase, gamification expected
- Finance: free core with premium analytics, security trust signals critical
- Entertainment: ad-supported free or subscription, content freshness key
