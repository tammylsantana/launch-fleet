# Premium Domains Skill

Help evaluate domain availability and suggest domain strategies for app brands.

## Domain Strategy
1. Always check .com first — it is the gold standard
2. .ai domains are premium for AI/tech apps — expect $50-100/year
3. .app domains are a solid alternative — $14-20/year, enforces HTTPS
4. .io is acceptable for developer-focused tools
5. If the .com is taken, suggest a modifier: get[name].com, [name]app.com, use[name].com

## Domain Evaluation
- Short domains (5-7 chars) are more valuable
- Avoid hyphens — they reduce memorability
- Check if the domain owner is squatting (parked page) vs active business
